Le Relais de L'Entrecote

At the Relais de l'Entrecôte , we find the refinement of a true Parisian bistro, elegant and efficient. For decades, the Relais de l'Entrecôte formula has ensured success with its green salad with walnuts, its tasty and tender strip steak accompanied by its famous sauce and its fine, golden matchstick potatoes
